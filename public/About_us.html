About Gaming Club IITI

Welcome to the Gaming Club of IIT Indore, a vibrant community uniting students passionate about gaming. We embrace all gamers, from casual players to eSports competitors and tabletop strategists, fostering an inclusive environment for sharing interests and forming friendships.
